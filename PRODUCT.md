# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Architects, interior designers, and visualization teams preparing client-facing 360 panorama presentations for residential, hospitality, and commercial spaces.

## Product Purpose

360 Render is a private panorama render studio that helps design professionals turn equirectangular room imagery into polished, immersive client presentations. Success means the homepage quickly communicates expert craft, visual confidence, and a protected workspace worth logging into.

## Brand Personality

Reserved, cinematic, exacting.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI SaaS templates, loud neon dashboards, warm gold/champagne accent systems, stock startup copy, crowded card grids, and playful consumer productivity aesthetics.

## Design Principles

- Show the space first: imagery and the interactive viewer should prove quality before claims do.
- Feel private and selective: the language should imply studio-grade access, not a mass-market template tool.
- Keep controls precise: actions, pricing, and workflow should feel clear, restrained, and professional.
- Let dark glass serve the product: glass surfaces should frame renders and controls, while the palette stays black, dark gray, and white so panorama imagery provides the color.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Prefer readable contrast, keyboard-visible focus states, reduced-motion support, and responsive layouts that keep the 360 preview usable on smaller screens.
